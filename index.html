Who We Are

Built on Decades of
Maritime Expertise

Coasta Sea Marine Services Private Limited is a Kerala-based marine services company with deep roots in the Indian maritime industry. Founded by experienced seafarers and maritime professionals, we deliver reliable, compliant, and cost-efficient services to shipowners, charterers, and offshore operators.

Our operations span major ports along India's western coastline, with a team of certified marine superintendents, port agents, and logistics specialists who understand the exacting standards of global maritime commerce.

What We Do

Comprehensive Marine Services

From a single port call to long-term crew management contracts, we deliver measurable value at every stage of your maritime operations.

Ship Agency

Full-service port agency for all vessel types — handling formalities, customs clearance, crew matters, and port disbursements with precision and speed.

  • Port clearance & documentation
  • Customs & immigration liaison
  • Husbandry services

Crew Management

End-to-end manning solutions covering recruitment, training, certification coordination, and deployment of qualified seafarers worldwide.

  • Global seafarer recruitment
  • STCW compliance management
  • Crew welfare & repatriation

Port Logistics

Coordinated cargo logistics, terminal handling, and freight forwarding services designed to minimize turnaround time and port costs.

  • Cargo supervision & tallying
  • Terminal & stevedoring coordination
  • Freight forwarding

Offshore Support

Logistics and vessel support for offshore oil, gas, and renewable energy projects — including supply runs, personnel transfer, and equipment handling.

  • OSV & AHTS management
  • Offshore project logistics
  • Safety compliance support

Ship Repair Coordination

Technical superintendence and drydock coordination for planned and emergency repairs, with a vetted network of shipyards across India.

  • Drydock project management
  • Vendor & shipyard liaison
  • Technical inspections

Marine Supplies & Provisioning

Complete vessel provisioning — stores, provisions, spare parts procurement, and bonded stores delivery to all major Indian ports.

  • Deck & engine stores
  • Fresh provisions & bonded stores
  • Spare parts sourcing
